# Requirement Analysis Checklist

Check identity and permissions, validation, states and transitions, business-rule precedence, idempotency, concurrency, time zones, localization, pagination and limits, timeout and retry, partial failure, auditability, data migration, backward compatibility, observability, rollout, rollback, analytics, accessibility, security, privacy, compliance, and operational ownership.

Apply only relevant checks. Do not inflate the output with inapplicable boilerplate.
